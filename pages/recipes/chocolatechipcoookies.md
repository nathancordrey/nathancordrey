# Chocolate Chip Cookies

Easy chocolate chip cookie recipe! (Can make 1/2 batch for quick weekday treat)
  
## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup light brown sugar
- 2 eggs 
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 cups chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Line 2-3 large baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, and salt). Set aside
3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients. Mix on low speed until combined.
5. Add chocolate chips and mix well.
6. Scoop 2-3 tbs of cookie dough into balls on parchment  paper.
7. Bake cookies for 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they just start to turn brown.
8. Allow to cool for 5 minutes on the cookie sheet then transfer to cooling rack.

### Enjoy!
