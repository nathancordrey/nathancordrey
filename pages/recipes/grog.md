# Grog

The standard Grog recipe.

## Ingredients

- 2 oz rum (Hatian Barbancourt 8 year preferred)
- 8 oz Water
- 1 tsp sugar
- 0.5 oz lime juice
- 1 squirt of tiki bitters


## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients, add Ice if preferred
2. Serve and Enjoy!
