# Overnight Oats (base)

Easy weeknight breakfast. Unlimited options for additional toppings!

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/4 cup yogurt
- 1 tbs chia seeds
- 1 tbs sweetener
- 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Put together
2. Leave in fridge overnight
3. Enjoy!
