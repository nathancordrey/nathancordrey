# Whiskey Sour

Ricky's Whiskey Sour Recipe!

## Ingredients

- 2 oz whiskey
- 3/4 oz lemon juice
- 3/4 oz simple syrup
- 1 egg white


## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Dry shake (shake with no ice) to aerate the egg white
3. Add Ice and shake over ice
3. Serve and Enjoy!
