A running prediction league for the {{ stats.title }} among friends. Before each match, everyone calls a winner and a scoreline. Points pile up, matchdays get trophies, and there's a side bet on every day's games. It's bragging rights, not a sportsbook.

Scoring

Every prediction is two calls: who wins (or a draw), and the exact scoreline.

+{{ scoring.correct_winner }}

+{{ scoring.exact_score_bonus }}
Exact score bonus
On top of the result point, for nailing the precise scoreline — worth {{ scoring.correct_winner + scoring.exact_score_bonus }} in total.

So getting the winner right is {{ scoring.correct_winner }} point; getting the exact score is {{ scoring.correct_winner + scoring.exact_score_bonus }}. Miss the result entirely and you get nothing for that game.

Reading the standings

The table is organised by the current matchday. Settled matchdays collapse to a single points number; the matchday in progress is broken out so you can see the race in detail.

MD1

✓
Games correct — winners called right in the current matchday.
🎯
Exact scores — precise scorelines nailed in the current matchday.
Pts
Matchday points — the current matchday's running total.

Trophies & the dunce cap

When a matchday is settled, its podium is immortalised next to the winners' names, and whoever finished bottom wears the clown.

🥇
Gold / silver / bronze — the top three of a completed matchday (🥈 🥉 too).
🤡
The clown — last place for the matchday. Better luck next round.
👑
Crown — whoever currently leads the live matchday.

The daily wager

Each day has its own little contest: most points across that day's games wins the day. The latest day's haul shows in its own column, and once every game that day is final, the winner takes the money bag.

💵
Today's column — points scored on the most recent match day.
💰
Daily winner — top scorer once the day's games are all complete (held back until then).

Making picks

Predictions can be sent any time before a match kicks off. Each pick is a winner and a scoreline — for example, “Brazil 2–1” or “1–1 draw.” Miss a game and you simply score nothing for it; you're never penalised for sitting one out.

Friendly reminder: this is a free, just-for-fun pool among friends — bragging rights and the occasional informal wager, nothing more.
